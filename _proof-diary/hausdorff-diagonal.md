**Key identity (for any open \(U,V \subset X\)):**

$$
(U\times V)\cap \Delta(X) \;=\; \{(z,z)\;:\; z\in U\cap V\}.
$$

Hence,

$$
\bigl((U\times V)\cap \Delta(X)\bigr)=\varnothing \;\Longleftrightarrow\; U\cap V=\varnothing.
$$
